Frequently asked questions

A point of sale (POS) system is a platform that businesses use to process and complete in-person customer payments. They are typically comprised of hardware and software.

You can accept the most popular payment types: credit cards, debit cards, gift cards, and contactless payments like digital wallets.

POS system pricing include a standard fee for each transaction. There's no monthly or startup fee—you pay only when you've been paid. Get more details about our fees.

Our POS system only works with the PayPal payment infrastructure. The POS terminal and other hardware accessories work with the PayPal’s POS software. You can also download the POS software for free on smartphones and tablets running on iOS 12 or Android 8 and higher.
